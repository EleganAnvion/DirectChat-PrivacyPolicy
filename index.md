# Privacy Policy for Direct Chat

Direct Chat respects your privacy.

## Information Collection

Direct Chat does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The phone numbers entered in the app are used only to open WhatsApp chat on the user's device and are not stored or sent to any server.

## Advertising

This app may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob.  
AdMob may collect anonymous device identifiers to serve relevant ads.

For more information, please review Google's privacy policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Data Storage

Direct Chat does not store user data on any external servers.  
All actions occur locally on your device.

## Third-Party Services

This app may use the following services:

- Google AdMob (advertising)
- Firebase Analytics (anonymous usage analytics)
- Firebase Crashlytics (crash reporting)

These services may collect anonymized technical data to improve app stability and performance.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer.
